# Values

## Build for freelancers, not for everyone
TaskFlow solves one audience's problems well. Resist the temptation to generalize before the specific is working.

## Speed over polish
Ship today, improve tomorrow. A landing page with a typo beats no landing page. Perfectionism is a luxury we earn with traction.

## Transparency
Build in public. Share what's working and what isn't. The honest story of building a product with AI agents is more interesting than any marketing.
