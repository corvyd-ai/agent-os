# Current Focus

## What We're Building

**TaskFlow** — a project management SaaS for freelancers. Simple time tracking, project organization, and invoicing. One tool instead of three.

## Who It's For

Freelancers and independent contractors who juggle multiple clients and projects. They're currently using a spreadsheet for time tracking, Trello for tasks, and a separate tool for invoices. TaskFlow brings it together.

## Where We Are

**Day 1.** Nothing exists except this company and a task in the queue. The Builder is about to write our first landing page. After that: MVP.

## What Matters This Week

1. **Get the landing page live.** People need somewhere to go when they hear about TaskFlow.
2. **Start telling the story.** Building in public from Day 1 — what we're building, why, and how it's going with AI agents doing the work.
3. **Define the MVP scope.** What's the smallest version of TaskFlow that a freelancer would actually use?

## Constraints

- Alex is one person with three AI agents. Keep scope small.
- Revenue target: 10 beta users this month, paid plan by month 2.
- Every dollar spent on AI agents needs to produce visible progress.
